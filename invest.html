Frequently Asked Questions

Is this an investment or a purchase?
Both. You're purchasing products at wholesale prices AND receiving revenue share as a bonus. It's structured as a business partnership, not a securities offering, so it's legally clean.
What if OVERKORE doesn't generate revenue?
You still keep the products (retail value 2x your investment). Worst case: break even by reselling. Best case: 4x your money with revenue share. We already have $22K revenue from $2.6K startup capital - demand is proven.
Do I get equity?
Founding Partners get products + revenue share, not equity. For equity investment ($50K+), we offer SAFE notes or direct equity at $2M post-money valuation.
How is revenue share calculated?
Simple: Gross monthly revenue √ó your percentage = your payment. If we do $50K/month and you're a Gold Partner (20%), you get $10K that month. Paid via ACH within 7 days of month end.
Can I resell the products?
Absolutely. Many Founding Partners plan to resell at retail prices, doubling their money immediately on products alone, then revenue share is pure profit.
What's the downside?
If we fail to generate revenue, you don't get revenue share payments. But you keep the products. If we succeed, you 2-4x your money. Risk/reward is heavily in your favor with proven Week 1 traction.
How many spots are available?
Limited to first 20 Founding Partners total. This keeps the group exclusive and ensures we can deliver exceptional returns and attention to each partner.
Is there a contract?
Yes. Founding Partner Agreement outlines all terms, revenue share structure, product delivery, and benefits. Legally binding. Fully transparent. No surprises.
