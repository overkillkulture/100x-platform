## WHY THIS WORKS

**Legal Right**: You're a party to the call = you have right to the recording

**U-Haul Disclosed**: They announce "calls are recorded" = they admit recording exists

**Discovery Alternative**: If they refuse, you can subpoena it in court (but this gives them chance to provide voluntarily)

**Creates Paper Trail**:
- If they provide it = you have evidence
- If they refuse = you document they're hiding evidence
- If they "lost it" = you document suspicious deletion
