## üìê THE 8 DIMENSIONS OF CRYPTOCURRENCY

Every successful cryptocurrency project has **8 interconnected dimensions**:

1. **Technical Architecture** - The blockchain and smart contracts (the foundation)
2. **Economic Design** - Tokenomics and incentive structures (the plumbing)
3. **Legal/Regulatory** - Compliance and entity structure (the permits)
4. **Social/Governance** - Community and decision-making (the neighborhood)
5. **Operational Execution** - Team and day-to-day operations (the construction crew)
6. **Marketing/Distribution** - Positioning and growth strategy (the sales/marketing)
7. **Data/Analytics** - Metrics and monitoring (the inspection system)
8. **Integration/Ecosystem** - Connections to broader crypto world (the utilities hookup)

**The breakthrough insight:** Most crypto projects fail because they focus on 1-2 dimensions (usually technical + marketing) and ignore the other 6. This bootstrap ensures you systematically address all 8.
