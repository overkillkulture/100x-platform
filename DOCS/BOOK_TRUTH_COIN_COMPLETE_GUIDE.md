## Chapter 14: Handling Crises - When Things Go Wrong

**Purpose:** Prepare for inevitable challenges

**Common Crypto Crises:**

**1. Smart Contract Bug**
- **Scenario:** Security vulnerability discovered
- **Response:**
  - Pause contract immediately (if pausable)
  - Public announcement within 1 hour
  - Emergency patch development
  - Re-audit before re-enabling
  - Compensation for affected users

**2. Market Crash**
- **Scenario:** Token price drops 80% in 24 hours
- **Response:**
  - Stay calm, communicate clearly
  - Emphasize long-term vision
  - Focus on product development
  - Community support (AMAs, updates)
  - Do NOT panic sell or manipulate

**3. Regulatory Action**
- **Scenario:** SEC/regulatory letter received
- **Response:**
  - Engage legal counsel immediately
  - Do not publicly comment until legal review
  - Compliance assessment
  - Remediation plan
  - Transparent communication (when legally safe)

**4. Community Revolt**
- **Scenario:** Community disagrees with major decision
- **Response:**
  - Listen first, defend second
  - Acknowledge concerns
  - Governance vote if appropriate
  - Compromise if possible
  - Accept outcome gracefully

**5. Competitor Copying**
- **Scenario:** Someone forks your token
- **Response:**
  - Focus on your moat (community, team, vision)
  - Accelerate development
  - Highlight differentiation
  - Welcome competition (proves demand)
  - Execute better

**6. Bad Press / FUD (Fear, Uncertainty, Doubt)**
- **Scenario:** Negative article or campaign
- **Response:**
  - Assess if legitimate or baseless
  - Respond factually, not emotionally
  - Correct misinformation
  - Ignore trolls
  - Focus on building

**Crisis Management Checklist:**
- [ ] Incident response team identified
- [ ] Communication channels ready
- [ ] Legal counsel on retainer
- [ ] Emergency pause mechanisms tested
- [ ] Community trust built BEFORE crisis
- [ ] Insurance (if available)
- [ ] Post-mortem process

**Exercise:**
- Identify top 5 risks for your token
- Write crisis response playbook
- Simulate crisis scenario

**Common Tokenomics Failures:**

**1. Too Much Inflation:**
- Tokens distributed faster than demand grows
- Price crashes
- Community loses faith
- **Fix:** Reduce emission rates, add burn mechanisms

**2. Too Much Deflation:**
- Tokens too scarce
- Hoarding behavior
- Low circulation/utility
- **Fix:** Increase rewards, reduce burn

**3. Poor Distribution:**
- Founders/VCs hold too much
- Community feels exploited
- Centralization risk
- **Fix:** Airdrop to community, vesting extensions

**4. Weak Utility:**
- Token not actually needed
- Artificial use cases
- Users prefer fiat
- **Fix:** Create REAL utility, integrate deeper
