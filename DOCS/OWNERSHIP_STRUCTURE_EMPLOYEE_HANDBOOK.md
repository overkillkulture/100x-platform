   - Payment = Acknowledgment of work-for-hire

4. **Legal Action (If Necessary)**
   - Breach of contract
   - Sue for damages if they try to compete
   - Enforce non-compete and NDA

**Prevention:** Clear agreements BEFORE employee starts work
