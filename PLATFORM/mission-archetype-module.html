Find Your Mission Archetype

Answer these questions honestly. One will resonate MOST:

üõ†Ô∏è Are you a CREATOR?

Core questions:

  1. Do you get restless when you're NOT making something?
  2. When you see a blank canvas/page/screen, do you feel excited (not anxious)?
  3. Do you think "I could build that" when you see products/tools?
  4. Do you prefer MAKING things over TALKING about them?
  5. Is your ideal day spent in deep work creating something?

If YES to 3+: You're likely a CREATOR

üåê Are you a CONNECTOR?

Core questions:

  1. Do you love introducing people who should meet?
  2. When you learn something valuable, do you immediately want to SHARE it?
  3. Do you get energy from facilitating collaboration between others?
  4. Do you prefer ORGANIZING people/resources over solo work?
  5. Is your ideal day spent connecting dots between people/ideas?

If YES to 3+: You're likely a CONNECTOR

‚ö° Are you a CATALYST?

Core questions:

  1. Do you see broken systems and feel COMPELLED to fix them?
  2. When you discover truth, do you need to WAKE OTHERS UP to it?
  3. Do you get energy from breakthrough "aha!" moments in others?
  4. Do you prefer INSPIRING change over building/connecting?
  5. Is your ideal day spent shifting how people think/act?

If YES to 3+: You're likely a CATALYST

üí° What if I score high on MULTIPLE?

- Creator PRIMARY, Catalyst SECONDARY = You build things that inspire change
- Connector PRIMARY, Creator SECONDARY = You organize people around what you create
- Catalyst PRIMARY, Connector SECONDARY = You inspire change by uniting people
