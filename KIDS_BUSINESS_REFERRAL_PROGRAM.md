## REFERRAL TRACKING SYSTEM

### HOW IT WORKS:

**Each kid gets unique referral code:**
- Format: `[KIT]-[FIRSTNAME]-[4 DIGITS]`
- Example: `JOY-AMELIA-2847`
- Example: `MILITIA-TYLER-8291`
- Example: `OBSERVER-SOFIA-1429`

**When someone uses the code:**
1. Stripe tracks it in metadata
2. Kid's referral dashboard updates
3. Commission calculated automatically
4. Payment sent monthly (or at threshold)

**Dashboard shows:**
- Total referrals made
- Total revenue generated
- Commission earned
- Leaderboard rank
- Next tier progress

**Kids can check ANYTIME:** `consciousnessrevolution.com/dashboard/[THEIR-CODE]`
