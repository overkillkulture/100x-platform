## TIPS FOR SUCCESS

**At the Hearing:**
- Dress professionally
- Arrive early
- Speak clearly and respectfully
- Address judge as "Your Honor"
- Have specific examples ready of why current counsel is inadequate OR why you're capable
- Show you've done research and are organized
- Don't complain excessively - focus on moving forward

**Judge Will Ask:**
- Do you understand you're giving up right to attorney?
- Do you understand complexity of your case?
- Do you understand you'll be held to same standards as lawyers?
- Are you making this decision freely?

**Your Answers:**
- "Yes, Your Honor, I understand and I'm making this decision voluntarily."
- "I've researched the procedures and I'm prepared."
- "I have organized all my evidence and I'm ready to proceed."
- "I believe I can best represent my own interests in this matter."
